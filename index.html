Welcome to PJ Environmental Consultants SDN. BHD.

"Greetings from PJ Environmental Consultants Sdn. Bhd., your committed environmental stewardship partner! Comprehensive Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA), state-of-the-art Industrial Environmental Treatment Solutions (IETS), and custom training programmes are our areas of expertise. Our goal is to equip organisations with sustainable practises so that business and the environment can coexist together. One small step at a time, we hope to create a greener tomorrow via our dedication to quality and innovation. Check out our offerings and come along on our path to a more environmentally responsible future!"

Objective

i. To assess the significant effects of your project or development proposal on the environment.

ii. To provide effective guidance and solution in environmental management for our clients.

iii. To provide extraordinary learning experience for industry personal in Environmental Management

Vision & Mission

To lead by example at PJ Environmental Consultants SDN. BHD., establishing new benchmarks for quality and creativity. By adhering to sustainable practices and constant improvement, we hope to positively affect our stakeholders, the environment, and society as a whole.

Our goal at PJ Environmental Consultants SDN. BHD. is to provide outstanding service that goes above and beyond for our clients. Our staff is committed to cultivating a culture of cooperation, diversity, and honesty. By utilising state-of-the-art technology, ethical business methods, and a customer-focused approach, our goal is to make a positive impact on the development and welfare of worldwide communities.
